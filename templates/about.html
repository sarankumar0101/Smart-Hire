About SmartHire

SmartHire is a modern, AI-powered job and internship portal designed to connect students and administrators with the best opportunities. Our platform streamlines the application process, offers advanced resume analysis, and provides a seamless experience for both job seekers and recruiters.

Key Features

Student & Administrator Registration
Secure sign-up and login for students and company administrators, with email verification and "Remember Me" support.
Job & Internship Postings
Administrators can post, manage, and review job and internship opportunities, while students can browse and apply easily.
AI Resume Analysis
Upload your resume and receive instant, AI-generated feedback on strengths, weaknesses, and suggestions for improvement.
Application Management
Track your applications, view statuses, and receive notifications about interview shortlisting or rejections.
Responsive Design
Enjoy a beautiful, mobile-friendly interface built with React and Tailwind CSS for a smooth experience on any device.
Secure & Private
All user data is securely stored and managed, with robust authentication and privacy controls.

Our Mission

To empower students and organizations by making the hiring process smarter, faster, and more transparent. We believe in leveraging technology to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity.
